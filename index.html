About Us

Theta Chi is a resolute Fraternity founded in 1856 on the principle of friendship through mutual assistance. It gives its members a fulfilling experience socially and academically preparing them in their lives after college. The Epsilon Psi Chapter continues to provide a helping hand to its community on and off campus. And we continue to break the negative stigmas of Greek life through events such as our annual car cutting for alcohol awareness week.
